Central Govt. Schemes

Here you will get category wise all government schemes.

Financial Assistance / Income Support

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

  • ₹6,000/year income support in 3 equal instalments
  • For small/marginal landholding farmers across India
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) into bank accounts
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

  • Monthly pension of ₹3,000 after age 60
  • Voluntary, contributory scheme for small & marginal farmers
  • Govt. co-contributes equal amount to pension fund
Apply Now

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

  • Pension of ₹1,000–₹5,000/month after 60 years
  • Open to all citizens (18–40 yrs); farmers encouraged to join
  • Contribution varies by age and desired pension
Apply Now

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) + Interest Subsidy (MISS)

  • Loan up to ₹3–5 lakh without collateral (up to ₹1.6 lakh)
  • 3% interest subsidy on timely repayment (effective rate: 4%)
  • Covers crop loans, animal husbandry, dairy, and fisheries
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

  • Crop insurance scheme for protection against crop loss
  • Premium: 2% (Kharif), 1.5% (Rabi), 5% (commercial crops)
  • Covers losses due to drought, flood, hailstorm, pests, etc.
Apply Now

Irrigation & Water Management

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

  • Promotes micro-irrigation (drip/sprinkler).
  • Funding for canals, tube wells, farm ponds, check dams.
  • Up to 55%–75% subsidy depending on region & category.
Apply Now

Per Drop More Crop (PDMC)

  • 55–75% subsidy for micro-irrigation systems.
  • Saves water, improves yield quality and quantity.
  • Reduces labor and fertilizer use.
Apply Now

Micro Irrigation Fund (MIF)

  • Enables state-level subsidy schemes for farmers.
  • Wider access to modern irrigation tech.
  • Promotes long-term water savings.
Apply Now

National Water Mission (NWM)

  • Awareness programs on water conservation.
  • Promotes efficient irrigation, reuse & water budgeting.
  • Encourages adoption of climate-resilient agri practices.
Apply Now

Market Access & Value Chain Support

e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

  • A pan-India electronic trading platform for agricultural commodities.
  • Benefits: Real-time price discovery, transparency, wider market reach.
  • Direct connectivity with 22,000 GrAMs for improved market reach
Apply Now

PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises

  • Helps small food processors (including farmer groups) with branding, tech, and training.
  • Benefits: 35% credit-linked subsidy, support for SHGs/FPOs.
  • Boosts shelf-life, income, and reduces post-harvest waste
Apply Now

Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs)

  • Village-level haats upgraded to include digital weighments and e-auction
  • Enhances local access, reduces farmer travel & dependence on middlemen
  • Linked to eNAM for wider market integration
Apply Now

Organic & Natural Farming

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

  • Promotes group-based organic farming in cluster mode.
  • ₹50,000/ha support for 3 years (₹31,000 directly to farmers).
  • Includes PGS certification which ensures premium prices and market access
Apply Now

Bhartiya Prakritik Krishi Paddhati (BPKP)

  • Focuses on natural farming using cow dung/urine (Jeevamrit, Bijamrit).
  • Offers ₹12,200/ha over 3 years for cluster formation, capacity building, certification, and residue testing
  • Reduces chemical input cost, enhances soil health.
Apply Now

National Mission on Natural Farming (NMNF)

  • Promotes chemical-free farming using Jeevamrit, Bijamrit & desi cow-based methods.
  • Support for training, demo clusters & adoption via FPOs/state agencies.
  • Active nationwide since 2022 (earlier BPKP restructured under NMNF).
Apply Now

MOVCDNER (Northeast Organic Mission)

  • Exclusive to Northeast India.
  • ₹10,000/ha for organic farming + support for branding, certification, and logistics.
  • Promotes complete value chain from farm to export.
Apply Now

Dairy, Poultry & Fishery Support

Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)

  • Support for desi cow breeding, artificial insemination, and IVF
  • Grants for Gokul Grams, mobile veterinary units, and animal tagging
  • Helps increase milk production and livestock quality
Apply Now

National Livestock Mission (NLM)

  • 50–100% subsidy for goat, sheep, piggery, and poultry units
  • Promotes backyard farming, feed & fodder development, and skill training
  • Focuses on rural youth, SHGs, and small landholding farmers
Apply Now

Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana

  • Financial assistance for ponds, hatcheries, cold storage, and boats
  • Subsidy for fish feed, transport, fish marketing, and processing units
  • Boosts employment, sustainable fishing, and income for fish farmers
Apply Now

Kisan Credit Card for Animal Husbandry & Fisheries

  • Get loan up to ₹3–5 lakh without collateral
  • Covers dairy, goats, poultry, inland fisheries, shrimp farming, etc.
  • Includes interest subvention on timely repayment
Apply Now
