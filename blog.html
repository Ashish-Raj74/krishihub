Know Your Land Rights

  • Right to Legal Land Title (Khatauni, Khasra, Patta)
  • You have the right to inherit family land.
  • You can access & update land records online
  • No one can sell your land without consent.
  • You can use land to get farm loans & benefits.

Farming Loss? Your Right to Financial Relief

  • Farmers can get loan waivers during droughts, floods, or crop failure
  • You must be registered with a bank or cooperative for eligibility
  • You can access & update land records online
  • State & central governments announce waiver schemes time to time.
  • You can check status or apply through CSCs or bank branches.

Marketing & Insurance for Every Farmer

  • Sell crops in mandis or directly to buyers.
  • Use e-NAM for online selling.
  • You can access & update land records online
  • Insure crops under PMFBY. Claim losses within 72 hours of damage.

Grievance Redressal ‚Äì Your Right to Be Heard

  • Farmers can file complaints for scheme delays or fraud.
  • Grievances can be filed at CSCs, Krishi Vigyan Kendras, or block offices.
